The Road to Retirement GFX package (01:18)

These graphics were developed for a financial education video series for participants of a retirement plan. Using a minimalist flat line icon style and an accompanying voice track, the series helps participants understand complex investment topics, as well as their benefits and responsibilities around saving for retirement.

Roles: Motion Graphics Designer, Storyboard Artist, Illustrator, Animator, Editor
