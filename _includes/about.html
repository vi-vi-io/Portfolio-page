About me

Hi, my name is Scott. I'm an independent creative, focused on interactive video—specifically, a deeper integration of code and video to deliver concise, memorable messages through a highly engaging, narrative, user-centered experience. My prior work includes videos and web apps for dozens of well-known, Fortune 500 corporate clients, city and state governments, and NGO's.

When I'm not behind a camera or a computer screen, I try to enjoy sailing, camping and other outdoor exploration, but most of my free time is spent renovating and restoring the historic seaside cottage where I live.

As project leader, or as just another cog in the machine, I bring this sense of adventure, a focused ingenuity, and roll-up-the-sleeves tenacity, to every project I work on.
