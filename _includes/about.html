About me

Hello, my name is Scott. I'm an independent video producer, with a focus on story, creating memorable messages through highly-engaging, narrative, user-centric experience. My prior work includes video production and web app development for dozens of well-known, Fortune 500 corporate, city and state government, and NGO clients.

When I'm not behind a camera or a computer screen, I look for opportunities to enjoy sailing, camping and other outdoor exploration, but most of my free time ends up being spent restoring the historic seaside cottage where I live. Not a bad tradeoff.

I bring this sense of adventure, focused ingenuity, and roll-up-the-sleeves tenacity, to every project that I work on.
