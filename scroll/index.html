The Voya Behavioral Finance Institute for Innovation

Effective retirement plan solutions are grounded in an understanding of the employees within the plan. And while surveys have been traditionally used to tell us the “who, what and why?”, we realize that what people say they are going to do—while well-intended—doesn’t always mirror actual behaviors.

The Voya Behavioral Finance Institute for Innovation is focused on improving retirement outcomes for plan participants by gaining a deeper understanding of the financial planning decision-making and behaviors of plan participants.

Behavioral Finance Institute for Innovation
How would a behavioral scientist look at your retirement plans?

Our Institute’s unique measurement system, known as the Reflection Index, enables you to make more informed plan design and communication decisions by better understanding how your retirement plan participants are making retirement decisions.

The Reflection Index is designed around the general consensus among behavioral economists that there are two modes of decision making: instinctive and reflective. Most behavior is governed by the instinctive system, such as when driving or hitting a baseball. Some behavior, however, engages the reflective system and is more slow and deliberate (such as when taking the SAT). Using these concepts, we’ve created a digital measurement system to diagnose how your plan participants have approached decision making activities in the last year.
